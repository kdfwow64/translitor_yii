Thank you for choosing ClientEngage Visitor Chat.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The ClientEngage Visitor Chat is a fully-featured real-time chat for your 
websites. And best of all: Visitor Chat comes with a Windows-based client 
out-of-the-box. Without having to visit the Visitor Chat’s admin-interface you 
will be notified from your Windows tray and can start chatting straight away!

By including one single snippet of JavaScript, this interactive and engaging 
Chat can be used on any website – whether you are using Wordpress, Joomla! 
or plain HTML.
